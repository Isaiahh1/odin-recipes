Fried Chicken

Recipe for Fried Chicken!

In todays recipe I will be discussing how to make some delicous, juicy and crunchy fried chicken in your air fryer at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (10 drumsticks)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2/3 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/3 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp celery salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried mustard
  • 4 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 3 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (optional for crispier skin)

Instructions

  1. Soak the chicken in buttermilk for up to 24 hours before starting.

  2. Place the eggs in one bowl and the seasoning in a seperate bowl. Stir the seasoning into the flour and whisk the eggs.

  3. Get the air fryer ready with a piece of baking paper on the surface for you to place the chicken on to.

  4. Take out soaked chicken pieces one at a time and dip them into the seasoned flour, then into the eggs and then back into the flour

  5. Begin placing the seasoned chicken pieces into the airfryer, try spacing them out if you can.

  6. Heat the airfryer at 200c for 10 minutes. Open up the airfryer, flip the pieces of chicken and then resume heating for another 10 minutes at 200c.

  7. Enjoy!
