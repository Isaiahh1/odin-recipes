Fried Chicken

Recipe for Fried Chicken!

In todays recipe I will be discussing how to make some delicous, juicy and crunchy fried chicken in your air fryer at home.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (10 drumsticks)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2/3 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp thyme
  • 1/2 tsp basil
  • 1/3 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp celery salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried mustard
  • 4 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 3 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp baking powder (optional for crispier skin)

Steps

  1. Soak the chicken in buttermilk for up to 24 hours before starting.

  2. Place the eggs in one bowl and the seasoning in a seperate bowl. Stir the seasoning into the flour and whisk the eggs.
